Prastavana (Introduction)

Gaay ek bahut hi upyogi aur shant pashu hai. Bharat mein gaay ko pavitra mana jata hai aur ise "Gau Mata" bhi kaha jata hai. Gaay manav jeevan ke liye bahut mahatvapurn hai.

Gaay Ka Varnan

Gaay ke chaar pair, do aankhen, do kaan aur ek lambi poonch hoti hai. Iske do singh hote hain. Gaay kai rangon ki hoti hai jaise safed, kaali, bhuri ya chittidar. Yah ghaas, bhoosa aur chara khati hai.

Gaay Ke Labh

Gaay hume poshtik doodh deti hai. Doodh se dahi, makkhan, paneer aur ghee banaya jata hai. Gaay ka gobar kheton ke liye acchi khaad ka kaam karta hai aur gobar se indhan bhi banaya jata hai. Gaumutra ka upyog kuch aushadhiyon mein bhi kiya jata hai.

Bharatiya Sanskriti Mein Mahatva

Bharatiya sanskriti mein gaay ka vishesh sthan hai. Kai tyoharon aur dharmik karyakramon mein gaay ki pooja ki jati hai. Log gaay ko daya aur seva ka pratik maante hain.

Upasamhār

Gaay ek bahut hi upyogi aur laabhdayak pashu hai. Hume gaayon ki raksha karni chahiye aur unki achhi dekhbhal karni chahiye. Gaay hamare jeevan aur krishi ke liye bahut mahatvapurn hai.
